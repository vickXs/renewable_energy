The Challenge

A Fragmented & Vulnerable Energy Landscape

Traditional backup configurations are disjointed, weather-dependent, and prone to rapid thermal degradation or catastrophic failure.

Severe Grid Unreliability

Frequent grid collapses force dependency on diesel generators, driving local operational OPEX up exponentially.

Thermal Degradation & Power Loss

When solar panels exceed 25°C, their energy output drops sharply. In high ambient heat climates, generation efficiency plummets by up to 15% with zero active mitigation.

Isolated Small-Wind Barriers

Small wind options remain complex, isolated, and unable to automatically communicate with existing home solar inverter schemes.

Catastrophic Fire Risk

Electrical faults in high-voltage DC string systems present real, unmonitored fire hazards. Current protection models are purely reactive.

The Solution

A Highly Engineered Hardware-Software Defense

Moving past simple power generation. Solarind actively monitors, regulates, and physically protects your energy infrastructure.

Dual-Source Redundancy

Continuous hardware integration of local Solar PV strings and a responsive micro-wind turbine input to maintain energy harvesting day and night.

Dynamic AI Balancing

Central microcontroller actively checks load demands, battery thermals, and generation profiles to optimize battery life cycle and prevent deep discharge.

Closed-Loop Protection

Automated pneumatic physical cooling drops panel temperatures instantly, paired with a localized physical dry chemical fire suppressant line that engages upon abnormal thermals.

Operational State Machine

Normal Mode

  • Panels harvest sunlight up to max rated kW.
  • Micro-wind turbine actively supplements input current.
  • AI-powered controller automatically switches load draw between solar, wind, battery, and grid power in real time.
  • Excess energy is channeled into balancing LiFePO4 packs.

Cooling Mode

Trigger: Temp > 45°C
  • Sensors detect rise in module back-sheet temperature.
  • Automatically drives active bottom-up air-cooling duct array.
  • Reduces peak panel temperature by up to 15°C under high loads.
  • Expected solar efficiency improvement: 12% to 18% during extreme weather.

Emergency Mode

Trigger: Smoke/Fault
  • Hard-disconnects solar arrays from power lines within <10ms.
  • Triggers localized high-expansion suppression deployment.
  • Completely suppresses hot spots before spread occurs.
  • Broadcasts localized telemetry and alert log to user cloud console.
